Pen & Pebble

A Small Town. Big Lessons.

1. A New Plan

Two friends decide to restore Riverside Park and make it useful for everyone.

2. First Obstacle

People doubt the project, but the friends choose to start with small steps.

3. Getting Help

A café owner joins the effort, proving teamwork makes difficult tasks easier.

4. Setback

A storm damages their progress, creating new challenges.

5. A Lesson Learned

They learn that setbacks are temporary and persistence matters.

6. Success

The park becomes a community reading nook and a source of pride.

Moral: Challenges will come and go, but teamwork, patience, and a positive mind can turn any dream into reality.
